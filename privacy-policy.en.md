# Treeg Privacy Policy
Last updated: February 19, 2026

Treeg is a personal expense tracking app.

## What data we process
We may process:
- account data (email, technical authentication identifiers);
- profile data you provide (such as name and avatar);
- data you create in the app (expenses, categories, goals, settings);
- technical data required for service operation and security.

## Why we process data
We use data to:
- provide app functionality;
- authenticate users and protect account access;
- sync and store user data;
- send notifications (if enabled by the user);
- comply with applicable law.

## Sharing and storage
We do not sell personal data.
To operate the service, we may use technical providers (e.g., Supabase, Apple, Expo).

## Account deletion
Account deletion is available inside the app.
After deletion, related user data is removed from active systems, except where retention is required by law.

## Contact
Privacy contact: treegteam@icloud.com

## Children
This app is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required by local law).
